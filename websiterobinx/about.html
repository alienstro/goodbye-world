About Me

Developer & Designer

Robinx Prhynz Aquino is a 19-year-old first-year student who is currently studying at Gordon College. He resides in Olongapo City and aspires to be a web developer in the future. He spends his time playing games and watching movies after classes.

He explored another course, which was Computer Science. a course that will lead him into greatness. Knowing the difficulties in this field, he still wanted to pursue this new career. With that being said, he welcomes you to his website.
